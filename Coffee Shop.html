TOCS(The One Coffee Shop)

About

Tocs is an upcomimg unlaunched business that aims
to provide a unique and exceptional coffee experience
to customers in Pretoria and Johannesburg. As a
Coffee Shop we understand the importance of a
good cup of coffee and strive to offer a diverse range
of coffee blends sourced from different parts of the world.
In addition to our coffee blends, we also offer high-quality teas,
sandwiches, and freshly squeezed juices to cater to the varying
preferences and dietary needs of our customers.


Origins

Tocs was founded with a passion for coffee and a desire
to create a welcoming space for individuals to enjoy their
favorite hot beverages. The idea for our business originated
from a personal love for coffee and a realization that there was
a gap in the market for a coffee shop that caters to the diverse
tastes and preferences of customers in Pretoria and Johannesburg.
With our expertise and dedication, we aim to establish Mlambo
Shop as the go-to destination for coffee enthusiasts and individuals
seeking a cozy ambiance to relax and unwind.
